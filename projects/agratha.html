AGRATA 2K25 is an electrifying national-level fest blending tech innovation and cultural vibrance. This fest celebrates talent, creativity, and collaboration.

Events include talent shows, dance battles, live music concerts, debates, quizzes, and competitive games like Battle of the Bands, Minute to Win It, and Extreme Sports Corner.

Interactive segments such as Treasure Hunts, Model United Nations (MUN), and a high-energy Coding Carnival engage a diverse student audience.

AGRATA 2K25 promises a dynamic atmosphere, unforgettable memories, and a platform to showcase the best of brains and vibes.
